Kyoka Hiroshima

Student

Kyoka Hiroshima is a first year student at the University of California, Riverside, pursuing a degree in psychology. Born and raised in Palo Alto, California, Kyoka is a dedicated and motivated individual who has excelled in both academics and work experience.

Kyoka attended Palo Alto High School and graduated in 2022 with high honors. During her time in high school, she was involved in various extracurricular activities such as the debate team, volunteering at a local community center, and playing on the varsity tennis team. She also volunteered as a tutor at her high school and helped students struggling with math and science.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, Kyoka continued her passion for teaching by working as a private tutor online. She helped students of all ages and backgrounds improve their grades and build confidence in their academic abilities. This experience allowed her to develop strong communication skills and adaptability in a constantly changing environment.

Kyoka also worked as a summer camp counselor in training at the Jewish Community Center (JCC) in Palo Alto. She was responsible for leading activities, supervising campers, and providing a fun and safe environment for children. Her natural leadership skills and ability to connect with children made her an asset to the camp team.

Most recently, Kyoka worked at Taste Buds Kitchen in Palo Alto for over a year. As a culinary instructor, she taught children and adults how to cook and bake in a fun and engaging way. She also managed the kitchen and ensured that all safety and hygiene standards were met. Her passion for cooking and love of teaching inspired many of her students to pursue their own culinary journeys.

Kyoka's work experience has equipped her with a diverse set of skills that make her an ideal candidate for any position. She is a quick learner, excellent communicator, and has strong problem-solving abilities. She is also highly organized, reliable, and adaptable to different work environments.

As a first year student at the University of California, Riverside, Kyoka is excited to continue her academic journey and explore new opportunities. She hopes to apply her knowledge and skills in psychology to help others and make a positive impact in the world.
